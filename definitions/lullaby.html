Lullaby

A bedtime song or chant used to put a child to sleep.


Japanese Lullaby. by Anonymous

Neneko, neneko, Ya!
Sleep, my little one, sleep!
As the bottomless pit of the ocean,
So is my love so deep!
Neneko, neneko, Ya!

Sleep, my little one, sleep!
As the unexplored vasts of Nirvanna,
So is my love so deep.

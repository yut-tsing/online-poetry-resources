Nursery Rhyme

A traditional poem or song or chant for children.


Ladybird, ladybird from Mother Goose

Ladybird, ladybird,
Fly away home,
Your house is on fire
And your children all gone;
All except one
And that's little Ann,
And she has crept under
The warming pan.

Links on poetry websites

Poets.org

Poets.org is produced by the Academy of American Poets, an online resource for poems, poets’ biographies, essays about poetry.
League of Canadian Poets

The League of Canadian Poets has a service called Poetry Pause, which sends a poem a day to subscribers. The archive of those poems is avaliable in their website.
Representative Poetry Online

Representative Poetry Online includes 4,700 English poems by 723 poets from Caedmon, in the Old English period, to the work of living poets today.
On Being

On Being was a podcast about the meaning of life. This is a link to the poetry archive of On Being, with both the poem text and the audio recording.
The Poetry Archive

The Poetry Archive is a charity dedicated to the production, acquisition and preservation of recordings of significant poets reading their work aloud.
Poetry Foundation

The Poetry Foundation is a private nonoperating foundation that aims to amplify poetry and celebrate poets. They have an exstensive digital archive.
Wordsfortheyear.com

Words for the Year is a project that publishes a poem or quote every day on its website.
Griffin Poetry Prize

The Griffin Poetry Prize is an annual literature prize awarded for a first-edition poetry collection written in, or translated into, English by a living poet and/or translator from any country. It has a archive of its poems of the week.
(the) curious nothing

(the) curious nothing is an arts collective and publisher of experimental mag/zines and books. This link goes to its list of featured poets and their works.
